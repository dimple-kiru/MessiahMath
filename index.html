About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate Mathematics educator, content creator, and curriculum developer with over six years of experience. Currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Mathematics at Vels University, Chennai, I hold M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees in Mathematics, both with First Class honors.

As the founder of Messiah Math EdTech Solutions, my mission is to transform complex mathematical concepts into clear, engaging, and accessible learning experiences. With a heart grounded in faith, I believe that every problem has a solution, and every student has the potential to excel when taught with purpose, creativity, and compassion.
